Building Excellence Since 1995

Founded by Eric Johnson in a small office with just three employees, Ericon Limited has grown into one of the most respected construction firms in the region. What began as a residential renovation company has evolved into a full-service construction firm handling projects of all scales and complexities.

Our growth has been fueled by an unwavering commitment to quality, integrity, and client satisfaction. We've never lost sight of our founder's original vision - to build structures that stand the test of time while exceeding client expectations at every turn.

Our History

A timeline of our growth and major milestones that have shaped Ericon Limited into the company it is today.

1995 - Company Founded

Eric Johnson establishes Ericon Limited as a small residential renovation company with just three employees.

Original office in downtown Building City

2002 - First Commercial Project

Completed our first commercial project - a 10,000 sq ft office building that marked our expansion into commercial construction.

Team grew to 15 employees

2010 - First Industry Award

Received the "Excellence in Construction" award for our innovative Green Office Complex project.

Annual revenue surpassed $10M

2015 - Industrial Division Launch

Expanded into industrial construction with the completion of a major manufacturing facility.

Specialized industrial team formed

2023 - Current Operations

Now operating with over 50 employees across three regional offices, handling projects up to $50M in value.

15 industry awards and counting
