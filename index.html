About Ericon Limited

Founded in 2010, Ericon Limited has grown from a small local contractor to one of the most respected construction firms in the region. Our commitment to quality, safety, and innovation has been the cornerstone of our success.

We specialize in delivering complex construction projects on time and within budget, while maintaining the highest standards of craftsmanship and client satisfaction.
